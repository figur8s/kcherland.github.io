The data, obtained from the <a href="https://archive-beta.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/drug+consumption+quantified"> UCI Repository for Machine Learning</a>, can also be found as <a href="https://doi.org/10.3886/ICPSR36536.v1">ICPSR 36536</a> via the Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research from the University of Michigan. The analysis is motivated by the desire to understand who is at greater risk of using and abusing specific drugs. The analysis identifies clustering of the data and creates models for prediction, specifically whether someone is a user of a drug in the past decade. See more details: <a href="https://github.com/figur8s/MLDrugStudy">.
